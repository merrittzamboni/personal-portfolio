Hi, I'm Merritt!

I'm a web designer, front-end developer, and accessibility advocate located in Albuquerque, New Mexico.

My passion for accessibility helps me create clear and informative designs. I aim to develop products that truly benefit their users by first communicating with and understanding the people themselves.

Currently, I have the opportunity to work on a wide variety of projects, ranging from a virtual conference website to branding for a podcast. I'm always looking to learn & grow while I build designs that put people first.
